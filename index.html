Meet The AB Team!

Adam

Owner & Executive Chef

Adam was born and raised in Wilmot. He created The Garden Stand in 2016, based on his passion for food. Adam makes customer satisfaction his top priority, bringing vision, creativity, and heart to every plate. Whether crafting exquisite dishes for a special occasion or sharing his love of seasonal flavours with the community, Adam is the driving force behind it all.

Kyle

Head Chef

Bringing unmatched skill, loyalty, and dedication to our team. Kyle is one of the hardest-working chefs you'll ever meet, he keeps the wheels turning and the team thriving. His craft and commitment elevate our menu every day, and we're genuinely honoured to have him at the heart of our kitchen.

Taylor

Culinary Lead & Kitchen Manager

Taylor moves seamlessly across every part of the business. From cooking and tackling catering orders to supporting the storefront and creating the content you see online. She jumps in wherever is needed to keep everything running smoothly.
